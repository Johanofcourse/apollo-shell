What's a "zone"? NWS doesn't issue heat alerts per county - it splits Florida into finer-grained forecast zones (coastal vs. inland vs. metro areas within the same county, for example "Coastal Broward County" and "Inland Broward County" are two separate zones). One advisory usually covers many zones at once, and a county can have more than one zone active while its neighbor has none - that's why the count on the dashboard can look larger than the number of counties actually affected.
