CUSTOMERS WITHOUT POWER RIGHT NOW
WHAT COUNTS AS A CUSTOMER?

One meter, one billing account — not one person, and not reliably one house. A single-family home is usually one customer, but a "1 customer" outage could also be an entire apartment building or strip mall on a single shared meter. Every utility we track reports at the meter level, not the person level.

WHY DOESN'T THIS MEAN A STORM IS COMING?

Florida's grid has small outages happening constantly — a car hits a pole, a squirrel gets somewhere it shouldn't. Most of what you see here is that ordinary background noise, not storm damage. The map's Historical Pattern view reflects each county's real long-run weather-match record, not just today's count.
